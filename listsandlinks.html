  1. Create a html file.
  2. Type in !doctype html at the top
  3. Add in meta charset="utf-8"
  4. Type in head and /head. Between the two, indent and type the first heading of the website.
  5. Inside head, type in title and /title. Indent the line between the two and type in the name of the website that will be displayed on the tab.
  6. Add in body and /body. Indent all the things added inside.
  7. Type in heaidngs and paragraphs as you see fit. Paragraphs are coded as p and /p and headers range from h1-h6.
  8. Once you've added all the headings and the paragraphs, close the body with /body.
  9. Add a footer by typing in footer and /footer. Indent the line in between and write what you want the footer to be.
  10. Once your html is to your liking, save your work.
  11. Save your work and go back to your terminal. Make sure you are in your repository folder.
  12. Type in sftp username@cmis-hsteacher
  13. Type in cd public_html
  14. Type in put insert-file-name
  15. Open a tab in your browser and type in cmis-hsteacher/~username/filename
  16. Once you've uploaded everything, type exit into your terminal to exit sftp.
